&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

Another way to express the same point is with (r , Œ∏)

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

With r being how far away the point is from the origin (radius) and Œ∏ being the angle from the x-axis to r.
