This chapter is all about an *open* economy. Remember, there are both closed and open economies. An open one simply means there is trading- so exports and imports.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

The first main thing you have to know is that the United States has an account called the current account. It keeps track of imports and exports, investment income like dividends, interest, rent or capital gains and transfer payments.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

You debit the account when you have outflows of foreign exchange and credit the account when you have inflow of foreign exchange.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

Simply,  
Money coming in = Credit the account  
Money coming out = Debit the account
